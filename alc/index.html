A Lost Connection

A young adult web comic available on Webtoon and Tapas
He loved her across oceans... now he'll chase her across lifetimes. An infamous 18th century pirate falls for a runaway concubine- but when she's captured and killed, he refuses to let go, and his quest for vengeance turns into a journey through time, immortality, and reincarnation. This isn't the end... it's just the beginning. How far would you go to find your soulmate? (Updates every Saturday 6pm)
